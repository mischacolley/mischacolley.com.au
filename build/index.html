Hi I’m Mischa Colley. I'm a Front-End Developer from the Northern Rivers region of NSW, Australia. I call Byron Bay home.

I’ve been building for the web since 2006. In that time I’ve worked for agencies such as Netengine, Orange Digital and Brand New Media along with my own clients through Exposé Creative. I'm currently looking for new employment opportunities.

Me Mischa Colley

I love writing HTML & CSS and I am passionate about building great user experiences.

Work

As a Front-End Developer the work I do in teams lies at the crossroads between design and development. I pride myself on my ability to work collaboratively with designers to bring designs to life in the browser and engineers to applying those designs to the underlying functionality and logic that makes websites and apps do what they do. I've done a lot of work with WordPress, Drupal and Ruby on Rails.

Life

I'm very passionate about the web work I do but I also have a life outside of work. I have a young son and love spending time with him, his mum and our network of friends and family. I love to travel and I have a passion for Education, Technology and the Creative Arts. Starting a family has brought a lot of my youthful desires for making the world a better place to the surface.

Business

My time spent building for the web has given me a diverse set of skills that enable me to help small business owners. Through my business, Exposé Creative, I help businesses interact and transact with their customers by providing knowledge and simple tools to make the web work for them. I pride myself on delivering simple solutions where people come before technology.

I believe strongly in the enabling power of the web, that being what drew me to it in the first place.
